Tastify

Stack: Google Maps API, Spotify API, Songkick API, Javascript, jQuery, CSS, and HTML.

Bloom

About: Bloom is a health and wellness app that was inspired by a form of journaling called Bullet Journaling. Bloom allows you to track different areas in your life that are considered to be integral in maintaining a positive lifestyle.

Pulse
